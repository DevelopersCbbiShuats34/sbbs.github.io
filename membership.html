Any person who has interest in the discipline of Bioinformatics and Biological sciences can become the member of the society. Life Members shall have voted right. Interested person shall apply on prescribed form and recommendation of Executive council is mandatory for becoming a Life member. Life member shall pay prescribed fee. Promises to abide by the rules and bye-laws and all the decisions taken by the Society in respect of the amendments of the rules and bye-laws. Subscribed in writing to carry out to the best of his/her capacity the objects of the Society.

There shall be three kinds of membership:

1. Annual member: Rs. 800/- as yearly subscription.
2. Life member: Rs. 7000/- as subscription.
3. Honorary member: Person who is scientist, educationist, industrialist and good worker of philanthropy may be appointed as honorary member of the Society.
